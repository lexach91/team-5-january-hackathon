How to meditate.

Let your mind wander.

Your mind is likely to wander which is completely natural. When this happens gently return focus to your breath ensuring your are inhaling and exhaling deeply.

Notice your body.

Make sure your body is in a stable position, one that you can stay in for a while. Take note of any aches and pains and accomodate for these in your meditation stance.

Set a timer.

If you are just starting on your meditation journey it can help to set a time limit to your sessions. It is recommended to start with around 5/10 minutes of mindfullness.

Avoiding Burnout

Give to others.

Add meaining to your work by giving to others. This can be small acts of kindness that will make you instantly re-energize you and give meaning to others.

Take control.

Find ways to find more autonomy in your work by taking control of tasks and projects. Ensure that this is a task you will enjoy and be able to easily accomplish.

Seperate your workspace.

Ensure that your workspace is seperated in your house to enable you to 'switch off' when you leave work. Treat closing the door to your study like leaving an office.
