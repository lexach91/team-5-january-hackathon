Create the Perfect Workspace

Proper Posture

Choosing a desk chair that will promote good posture. Preventing back pain can help you work and focus for longer.

Good Lighting.

Don't underestimate the impact light has on work rate. Ideally you should aim for indirect light that doesnt reflect off your computer monitor.

Clean Aesthetic.

Clean your workspace before starting work. Get plants to increase the appearance of your workspace and the your overall mood.

Organisation is Key

Make Lists.

Lists can help you prioritise tasks and ensure that you remain focussed on the overall goal.

Delegate Tasks.

Assign tasks to others to reduce your workload and increase team productivity. This can help improve focus on manageable areas of work.

Reduce Clutter.

Keep only important items on the top of your desk. Archieve any resources your rarely use and clear your inbox of junk mail.
